Emperor Penguin

They mate with one couple for a season, but the next year they will probably mate with another penguin.

  • Scientific name:Aptenodytes forsteri
  • Average Length: 1.2 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Antarctic Continent

Emperors are the largest of all penguins.These flightless animals live on the Antarctic ice and in the frigid surrounding waters. They are mostly known for their parenting. While the male penguin protects the egg, the female penguin goes to find food and returns with a full belly.
