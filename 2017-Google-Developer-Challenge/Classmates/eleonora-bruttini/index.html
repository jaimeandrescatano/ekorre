Shadow Dragon

Legendary

"I breathe nightmares."

  • Element: shadow
  • Unique ability: furiousness
  • Lair: Onirio
  • Weak against: light, sacred

Born out of fears, Shadow Dragon induces nightmares into its preys' minds. Upon attack, a minor creature must be sacrificed to its fury.
